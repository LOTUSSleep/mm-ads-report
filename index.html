Staff Concern

Are We Showing for "Mattress Toppers" & "Air Mattresses"?

What We Found

We are NOT directly bidding on "mattress topper" or "air mattress" keywords. These terms are not in any of our 797 active keywords.

However, our Performance Max campaigns and broad match keywords can trigger ads on related searches that Google's algorithm considers relevant. When someone searches "mattress topper saskatoon," Google may show our PMax ad because it contains the word "mattress."

Why This Matters

Staff are seeing customers come in expecting products we don't carry. This wastes ad spend and creates a poor customer experience. We're paying for clicks from people who want mattress toppers or air mattresses — products we don't sell.

Immediate Fix: Add Negative Keywords

Add these as account-level negative keywords today:

mattress topper air mattress inflatable mattress mattress pad camping mattress air bed crib mattress futon mattress cover used mattress second hand mattress recycling mattress disposal free mattress

This blocks these searches across ALL campaigns instantly.

Live Site Audit — What's Missing

Current Site: Gaps to Fix

These are the issues we found that will hurt campaign performance if not addressed before the new site launches.

Critical Gap

No Individual Store Pages

All 6 location links in the footer point to the same /pages/locations-landing page. Saskatoon North, Saskatoon Rosewood, Regina POW, Regina Rochdale, Moose Jaw, Yorkton — none have their own URL.

Why it matters: Google Ads location campaigns need unique landing pages per store for Quality Score and local relevance.

Critical Gap

No Size-Specific Landing Pages

No pages for /king-mattress, /queen-mattress, or /twin-mattress. Size keywords are trending +14% and we're bidding on them — but they all land on the generic all-mattresses page.

New site needs: dedicated size pages that filter collections by size availability.

High Gap

No Rochdale Outlet Page

The Rochdale outlet location is mentioned in ad copy but /pages/rochdale-outlet returns 404. Campaign references an outlet experience that doesn't exist online.

High Gap

No Comparison / Why Us Page

/compare-mattresses and /why-modern-mattress don't exist. Competitor campaigns (Sleep Country) need a landing page that clearly differentiates us. The homepage touches on it but doesn't give it enough space.

High Gap

Locations Page Has No Google Maps Embed

The locations page lists all 6 stores with addresses and phone numbers, but uses simple Google Maps text links. Interactive map embeds would improve user experience and increase "Get Directions" conversion rate.

Nice to Have

No Discovery Experience Page

The in-store Discovery process (pressure mapping) is Modern Mattress's biggest differentiator. A dedicated /discovery-experience page would serve brand campaigns and give PMax better content to work with.
